Mode 1

Study new words into categories.

  • 1
    Select Language

    BlaBoo supports multiple languages. Press the Change Language button at the top of the Home screen to select the language you want to study.

  • 2
    Select Category

    Select the category in which you want to study the word. BlaBoo support 15 categories such as Animals, Occupations, Vehicles, Food, and more.

  • 3
    Touch For Pronouncing!

    Volume Up! touch the illustrations on the screen. You can hear the voices of the words of the illustrations.

Mode 2

Review with word cards

  • 1
    Select Language

    BlaBoo supports multiple languages. Press the Change Language button at the top of the Home screen to select the language you want to study.

  • 2
    Review

    You can use the word card to review the words you studied. When you slide the screen, the next word appears.

  • 3
    Study Pronunciation

    If you want to check the pronunciation of a word, you can hear the word again by pressing the speaker mark.
